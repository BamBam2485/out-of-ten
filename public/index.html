Out of Ten
A party game of ratings & deception
How to play
Step 1 — Join a lobby
One person creates a lobby and shares the 4-letter code. Everyone joins on their own phone.
Step 2 — Roles are assigned
Everyone gets a secret rating (1–10). One player is the Impostor — they don't see the rating.
Step 3 — Statements
Take turns typing a statement that fits the rating. 30 seconds each. The Impostor has to bluff.
Step 4 — Vote
Discuss, then vote on who you think is the Impostor. Wrong vote = Impostor wins.
Impostor special rule
At ANY point — during statements, discussion, or vote — the Impostor can guess the exact rating. Correct = instant win. Wrong = instant loss.
